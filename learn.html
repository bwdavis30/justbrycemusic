Learn

Technique

Technique forms the physical foundation of drumming. Learn the essential methods that allow you to play effectively and confidently. As the most important aspect of rudimental drumming, the discovery of technique will prepare you for the progression into further advanced concepts.

[Learn Technique]

Notation

Notation refers to the fundamentals of percussion music. Discover these fundamentals and the skills applied directly to drumming. Discover how to interpret the unique structure, symbols, and rhythms of drum notation so you can accurately and easily read common percussion writing.

[Learn Notation]

Rhythm

Rhythm introduces the core timing concepts that shape drumming. Learn the basic and advanced methods of rhythm. As this section marks your transition into more advanced rhythmic skills of drumming, challenge yourself to develop high-end strengths for ease of progression.

[Learn Rhythm]

Rudiments

Rudiments are essentially the vocabulary of drumming. Explore the fundamental patterns while progressively challenging yourself with material designed to build your personal skills in drumming. Step into the complex world of percussion by learning and perfecting the necessary rudiments required for drumming.

[Learn Rudiments]

Practicing

Practicing teaches you how to retain, apply, and refine information you have learned. Discover effective strategies for developing growth in your personal skills. Learn through the paved process of how to efficiently practice drumming skills of all difficulties.

[Learn Practicing]
